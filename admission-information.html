What You Need

Admission Requirements

Please ensure you have the following documents ready before starting your application

Essential Documents

  • Birth Certificate (Original & Copy)
  • Transfer Certificate from previous school
  • Previous year's report card
  • 4 passport-size photographs
  • Address proof (Electricity bill / Aadhar)

Parent / Guardian Details

  • Father's & Mother's Aadhar Card
  • Parent photographs (2 each)
  • Occupation & qualification details
  • Valid email ID & phone number
  • Emergency contact information

Age Criteria

  • Nursery: 2.5 to 3.5 years
  • LKG: 3.5 to 4.5 years
  • UKG: 4.5 to 5.5 years
  • Class 1: 5.5 to 6.5 years
  • Age calculated as on March 31st

Health & Medical

  • Medical fitness certificate
  • Immunization records (if applicable)
  • Blood group information
  • Any special medical conditions (if any)
  • Allergies or dietary restrictions
