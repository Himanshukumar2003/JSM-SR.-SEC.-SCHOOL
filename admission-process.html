Offline Mode

Offline Admission Process

Visit our campus during working hours to complete the offline admission. Our counselors will guide you from form collection through document verification to final confirmation.

  • 1
    Visit the Campus Office

    Come during working hours — Monday to Saturday, 08:30 AM to 03:30 PM.

  • 2
    Purchase the Admission Form

    Collect the admission form from the admission counter at campus.

  • 3
    Carefully Fill In All Required Details

    Fill all personal, academic, and contact details accurately and legibly.

  • 4
    Attach the Necessary Documents

    Attach photocopies of all required documents as specified in the form checklist.

  • 5
    Submit the Completed Form Within 3 Days

    Return the filled form to the admission counter within 3 days of purchase.

  • 6
    Document Verification

    Documents will be reviewed and verified by the Admission Department.

  • 7
    Admission Confirmation

    Upon successful verification and eligibility approval, your admission is confirmed.

Documents Required
Carry photocopies of the following documents
  • Passport-size photographs (recent)
  • Previous academic mark sheets & certificates
  • ID proof (Aadhar Card / Passport / Voter ID)
  • Address proof
  • Category certificate (if applicable)

Online Mode

Online Admission Process

Apply easily from anywhere through our official website. The online portal lets you complete the entire process from home — quickly, securely, and without any hassle.

  • 1
    Visit the Official Website

    Open the university's official website on any device — mobile, tablet, or desktop.

  • 2
    Navigate to the Admissions Section

    Go to the Admissions menu and locate the current session's application page.

  • 3
    Click on Online Registration

    Click the "Online Registration" link to begin creating your applicant account.

  • 4
    Fill Out the Application Form

    Enter all personal, academic, and contact information accurately in the form.

  • 5
    Upload All Required Documents

    Upload scanned copies in the specified format and file size limit.

  • 6
    Review All Details Carefully

    Double-check all entered information and uploaded documents before proceeding.

  • 7
    Submit the Application Form

    Click Submit — an acknowledgement will be sent to your registered email address.

Verification & Confirmation
  • Details entered in the form will be verified against the uploaded documents by our admission team.
  • If eligible, a confirmation email will be sent within 24–48 working hours of submission.
  • If the applicant does not meet eligibility criteria, an email notification will be sent accordingly.
  • All formalities must be completed within 3 days of receiving registration confirmation.
  • Keep your login credentials safe for future status tracking on the portal.
Online Application Tips
  • Upload documents as clear, readable scans — PDF or JPEG preferred
  • Use a valid, active email address for all correspondence
  • Ensure a stable internet connection while filling and submitting the form
  • Save your application number after submission for future reference
