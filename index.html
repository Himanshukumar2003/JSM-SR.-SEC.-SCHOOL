How to Apply

Follow these simple and clear steps to successfully complete the admission process and secure your child’s place at our school. Each stage is designed to ensure a smooth, transparent, and hassle-free enrollment experience.

01
Fill the Application Form

Collect the application form from the school office or download it from the website. Submit the completed form within the given timeline.

02
Admission Test & Result

Eligible students will appear for the admission test (if applicable). The result will be communicated by the school office.

03
Document Verification & Fee Payment

Submit the required documents for verification and complete the fee payment to confirm admission.
