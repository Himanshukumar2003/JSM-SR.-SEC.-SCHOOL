About Ongoing Classes

At JSM SR. SEC. SCHOOL, regular and well-structured classes are conducted to ensure continuous academic progress of students. Our ongoing classes focus on building strong conceptual understanding through interactive teaching methods and consistent practice.

Teachers carefully plan lessons according to the academic curriculum and ensure that every topic is explained with clarity and real-life examples. Students are encouraged to actively participate, ask questions, and engage in discussions to strengthen their understanding.

Regular assessments, class activities, and revision sessions are conducted to monitor progress and improve performance. Our goal is to maintain academic discipline while creating a positive and motivating classroom environment.
