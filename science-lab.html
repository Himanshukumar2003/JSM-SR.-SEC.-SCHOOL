About Our Science Lab

The Science Laboratory at JSM SR. SEC. SCHOOL is designed to provide students with hands-on practical knowledge that complements classroom learning. Our lab is well-equipped with modern instruments, scientific models, safety equipment, and experiment materials that allow students to understand scientific concepts through real-life experimentation.

We believe that science is best learned by doing. Our laboratory encourages curiosity, observation, and innovation among students. Under the guidance of experienced faculty members, students perform experiments safely and develop analytical and problem-solving skills that prepare them for higher education and competitive examinations.

The lab environment is structured to maintain strict safety standards while ensuring interactive learning. Regular practical sessions are conducted for Physics, Chemistry, and Biology to enhance conceptual clarity and scientific temperament among students.
